About Me

Self-Interests & Hobbies

I love exploring Duluth's unique beaches and natural wonders. Black Sand Beach is one of my favorite spots for photography and relaxation.

Photography is one of my passions, especially capturing iconic Duluth landmarks like the Aerial Lift Bridge during different times of day.

Winter hiking along Lake Superior is incredible. I enjoy documenting the amazing ice formations that form along the shoreline.

Kayaking on Lake Superior gives me a unique perspective of the city and helps me stay active while enjoying nature.
